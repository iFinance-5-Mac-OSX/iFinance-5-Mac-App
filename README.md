## Overview  

iFinance 5 for macOS is a professional-grade personal finance application that empowers users to track, manage, and analyze all aspects of their finances. The software provides intuitive tools for monitoring income, expenses, investments, and budgets, helping users make informed financial decisions.  

With iFinance 5, you can manage multiple accounts, categorize transactions, and create visual reports to easily understand spending patterns and income trends. It supports importing bank statements and CSV files, simplifying the process of keeping your finances up to date.  

The app is fully optimized for macOS, including Apple Silicon and Intel Macs, delivering fast performance and a smooth user experience. Its interface combines clarity and functionality, making it suitable for both beginners and advanced users who need detailed control over their financial data.  

Whether tracking daily expenses, planning monthly budgets, or reviewing long-term financial goals, iFinance 5 offers a robust and flexible solution to manage your money effectively on Mac.
